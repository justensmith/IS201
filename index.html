Passions and Hobbies

I am a firm believer that what people do defines who a person is. That being said,in order to know me, you have to know what I am passionate about. In my life, there are really only four things that I am passionate about...my family, my faith, sports, and nature. I am who I am today because of my passions, and I love the things that I do.

Some of my hobbies include football, baskteball, soccer, pickleball, hiking, boating, fishing, spending time with my friends and family, going to concerts, vacationing, watching netflix, eating, and so much more. These are some of my favorite things to do in my free time, but there are many other things I enjoy doing.

  • Honesty and Integrity - To me, it is more important to be trusted than it is to be loved. Honesty and Integrity are important to me and my work.
